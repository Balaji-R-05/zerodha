Frequently asked questions

What documents are required to open an account?

You need your PAN card, Aadhaar card, and a cancelled cheque or bank statement. Aadhaar must be linked to your mobile number for E-sign.

Are there any annual maintenance charges (AMC)?

Zerodha charges a nominal AMC of ₹300 per year, billed quarterly, to maintain your demat account.

How long does it take to open an account?

The online process takes about 15 minutes. Once submitted, your account is typically activated within 24-48 working hours.
